Frequently Asked

CleanTrail™, answered.

The questions our customers, property managers, and insurers ask most often.

What is VM CleanTrail™?
VM CleanTrail™ is the digital proof system included with every Vacu-Man duct cleaning service. After your appointment, a QR-coded sticker is left on your furnace. Scanning the code opens your property-specific service record with before and after photos, a full record of work completed, and a dryer vent certificate of completion if applicable.
Does Vacu-Man take before and after photos of duct cleaning?
Yes. Every Vacu-Man duct cleaning includes documented before and after photos delivered through VM CleanTrail™. Our technicians photograph the supply trunk, return trunk, individual ductwork, and the blower fan, then upload the images to your customer portal so you can verify the work the same day.
How do I see proof that my ducts were actually cleaned?
Scan the QR code on the Vacu-Man sticker placed on your furnace after service. The CleanTrail™ portal opens directly to your property-specific service record, including timestamped before-and-after photos, the technician who performed the work, and a downloadable record.
Do you provide a certificate of completion for dryer vent cleaning?
Yes. Every dryer vent cleaning is paired with a downloadable certificate of completion through VM CleanTrail™. Property managers, condo boards, and insurance providers commonly request this document, and it is available on demand from your portal.
How long has Vacu-Man been in business?
Vacu-Man Furnace and Duct Cleaning Services was founded on August 27, 1980 by Stephen Oldfield. The company is family-owned, currently led by Adam Oldfield as President and Michelle Oldfield as Vice President, with 45 years of continuous operation across the Greater Hamilton Area.
What areas does Vacu-Man service?
Vacu-Man services Hamilton, Burlington, Oakville, Mississauga, and Brantford for residential and commercial duct cleaning, furnace cleaning, and dryer vent cleaning.
Is VM CleanTrail™ free for Vacu-Man customers?
Yes. CleanTrail™ access is included at no additional charge with every Vacu-Man service. There is no app to download, no account to create, and no subscription. Scan the sticker to view your records.
Does VM CleanTrail™ show my personal information?
No. VM CleanTrail™ is designed to show service documentation, not private customer information. Your page may include service photos, service dates, and completion records, but it does not display customer names, payment details, phone numbers, or billing information.
What makes Vacu-Man different from other duct cleaning companies?
3 things set Vacu-Man apart. First, 45 years of family-owned operation in the same community. Second, our motto, Do It Right or Don't Do It At All, applied to every clean. Third, VM CleanTrail™, our verified proof system that documents every job with photos, history, and certificates accessible the same day.
